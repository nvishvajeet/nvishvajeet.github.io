Qubit

A quantum technologies working group at MIT World Peace University, Pune.

Qubit is an early-stage working group bringing together faculty from physics, computer science, mathematics, and electronics around a shared programme in quantum technologies. Our near-term goal is to set up an undergraduate quantum laboratory at MIT-WPU, and to grow a small research effort around it — aligned with the four verticals of the National Quantum Mission: quantum computing, communication, sensing, and materials.

The group was constituted in 2025 as a project under the DST Quantum Technology Cell. We are actively building — curriculum, infrastructure, and collaborations — rather than reporting finished work. Expect this site to grow as the group does.
