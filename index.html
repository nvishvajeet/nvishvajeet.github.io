I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Rutgers University, where I am advised by Prof. Swastik Kopparty. My interests broadly lie in Complexity Theory, and I'm a part of the Theory CS group here. Previously, I did my bachelor's and master's degree at IIT Madras where I worked with Prof. Radhakrishna Ganti for my master's thesis. I was fortunate to have interned with Prof. Prahladh Harsha at TIFR Mumbai and Dr. Satya Lokam at Microsoft Research, India during my time as an undergraduate student.
