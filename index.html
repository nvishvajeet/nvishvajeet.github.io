    I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Rutgers University, where I am a part of the Theory CS group. I am extremely fortunate to be advised by Prof. Swastik Kopparty, who is now at the University of Toronto.

I also had the privilege of interning with Prof. Prahladh Harsha at TIFR, Mumbai and Dr. Satya Lokam at Microsoft Research, India during my time as an undergraduate student.
